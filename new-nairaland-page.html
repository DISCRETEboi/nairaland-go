Share Your Unemployment Experience - Jobs/Vacancies - Nigeria posted by Threesha
I have been laid off once and fired once. And I also survived 2 mass layoffs.

None of those experiences felt good. When I was let go the first time, I panicked because I had no savings.

I spent 3 months unemployed.

The second time, I had savings and spent 5 months unemployed. But I survived both times and actually moved into better roles.

Today, I’m grateful that I had those experience even though I was devastated in those moments.

They pushed me to the next level in my career each time. To everyone being affected by layoffs, please take the time to breathe.

Evaluate what you really want out of your next opportunity.

And keep your head up.

Something better is coming!

I came back wounded. Tortured. Sad. Missing parts of me. And I wasn’t prepared for that loss.

But, are we ever prepared for ANY loss?

It is human (and foolish) of us to claim anything as ours. We clench to and protect things, people, whatever with supreme fear of losing them when in reality we know deep inside that nothing ever belongs to us. Even the air we breathe is borrowed. Our children are borrowed. The time we spend here on earth is borrowed.

Life has a way of reminding us of this truth to keep us humble, and grateful.

So here I am. Grateful and humbled. I’m here to remind you that everything around you is a gift. Enjoy it as long as it lasts. Never take it for granted.

And when the time comes to let go of it, don’t hold tight. Simply open your heart, mind and hands and release it with grace and gratitude that it was once under your care.

It is only when you release what was that you are able to receive what will be.
